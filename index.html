Grader's Note: I have created a scene that fulfils all Assignment 5A's and 5B's rubic points along with adding the following bonus features:

  1. In the large vehicle, it contains textures from real Coca-Cola trucks and products.
  2. The road and grass, textured from their real life counterparts, covers the entire scene.
  3. The default origin of the directional light is from a spinning sphere in the sky, which is called the Sun.
  4. All vehicles are spaced out, just like in real life when the road is not clogged with a traffic jam.
  5. The houses, trees, tables, and chairs are all spaced out evenly, just like in a real-life suburb.
  6. All spheres in the scene are animated to spin.
  7. The lights and camera are fully customizable with a given GUI.
  8. The default colors of the hemisphere light are sky blue and grass green, which mimics their real-life counterparts.
  9. The code is structured in a way that makes development easier by splitting all code into its own seperate sections.
  10. Every non-textured object has a specular reflection.
  11. The scene's lights are the very weak ambient light, the strong directional light, and the weak hemisphere light intended to be similar to real life.
  12. The default color of the directional light is sunlight, which mimics its real-life counterpart.
  13. The default color of the ambient light is white, which mimics its real-life counterpart.
